# 🎩 How the Hat Works (Explained for a 2-Year-Old!)

Imagine you have a magical talking hat! 🧙‍♂️✨

Here is what the hat has inside it:

### 1. The Brain 🧠 (Raspberry Pi)
Inside the hat is a tiny, smart computer. It thinks very fast and tells all the other parts what to do! It is the boss of the hat.

### 2. The Eyes 👀 (Camera & Sensors)
- **The Camera** looks at what is in front of you. It can see trees, people, and read signs!
- **The Bat Ears (Sensors)** send out invisible sound waves that bounce off walls. If a wall is too close, the ears hear the echo and secretly tell the Brain!

### 3. The Voice 🗣️ (Speaker)
The hat can talk to you! If the bat ears hear a wall getting super close, the hat uses its Voice to go *"BEEP BEEP!"* so you don't bump your head. If the camera sees a dog, the hat uses its Voice to say *"I see a dog!"*

### 4. The Magic Buttons 🖲️ (Wrist Pad)
You wear a cool bracelet with 4 big buttons. You press a button to ask the hat a question!
- **Press Button 1:** "Hat, what do you see right now?"
- **Press Button 2:** "Hat, can you read that sign for me?"
- **Press Button 3:** "Hat, turn on the bat ears so I don't bump into things!"
- **Press Button 4:** "Hat, can you say that one more time?"

---

## 🔄 How They Play Together!
When you press a **Magic Button**, the **Brain** wakes up! It asks the **Eyes** to take a quick peek. The Brain thinks about what the Eyes saw, and then it uses the **Voice** to whisper in your ear so you can walk safely and happily! 🚶‍♂️🎉
